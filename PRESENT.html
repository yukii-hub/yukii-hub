ABSENTEEISM?

Absenteeism is a serious issue that affects productivity, morale, and costs in workplaces and schools. While some absences are unavoidable, chronic absenteeism can often be prevented through effective policies, supportive leadership, and employee well-being programs. By addressing the root causes and fostering a positive environment, organizations can reduce absenteeism and improve overall performance.
